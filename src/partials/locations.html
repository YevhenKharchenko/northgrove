The Journey Map

Follow the Path Through a Living Landscape

The progress feels better because you're moving through this actual map instead of just a boring level menu You move through winding roads, green spaces, ponds, and little countryside details that make each step feel connected to the last one.
It makes the whole thing feel more like you're actually going somewhere, discovering stuff. Unlocking a new area feels less like opening another screen and more like continuing a quiet adventure. You get into this nice flow of solving puzzles and then seeing what's next on the map
