About the Game

A Garden World Built Around Small Joys

So Grove Plot basically takes your typical match-3 setup but drops it into this world that actually feels warm and lived-in. Instead of abstract icons and flat boards, you work with apples, blueberries, lemons, and flowers that look like they belong in the world around them. It's not just throwing levels at you - it's giving you an actual place to be in.
And honestly? That's what stuck with me. You are not only solving puzzles but moving through a gentle landscape that feels handcrafted, bright, and easy to return to. The adorable wolf companion adds even more personality, making the whole experience feel friendlier and more grounded.
